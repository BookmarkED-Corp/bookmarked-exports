Key Strategic Insights

SLJ AI Constraint is HARD

"Anything that smacks of AI would be a major red flag for our brand." Do NOT pitch AI reviews to SLJ editorial. Hold until Phase 1 is proven and trust is established.

40% of Purchased Books Never Circulate

Powerful data point for librarian messaging. Data-driven purchasing is a compelling value prop.

Nonfiction Review Gap is Real

"Nonfiction books never get reviews" — but districts still require 2–3 reviews per title. If Bookmarked is accepted as a review source, that's a game-changer.

Districts Are Already Doing DIY Versions

At least one district contact is manually using ChatGPT + spreadsheets for SB13 screening. Exact product-market fit validation.

Revenue Share Not Formalized

Steve offered to share fee-based review revenue with SLJ. No terms formalized. Formal agreement required before any publisher referrals.

PEN America Data is "Completely Inaccurate"

Districts are blanket-rejecting entire lists without nuance. Bookmarked's network-effect data can credibly address this by providing verified, granular tracking.
