ESC 16 Partnership Terms

Proposed Agreement — March 2, 2026
Proposed partnership terms between Bookmarked and Education Service Center Region 16. Flat revenue share, minimum guarantee, and step-downs at scale. Retroactive to January 1, 2026.

Financial Terms

Revenue Share
10% of Texas revenue flowing through ESC 16 contracting services
Minimum Guarantee
$75,000 per year. If 10% of annual revenue falls below $75,000, Bookmarked will pay the difference to bring ESC 16's total to $75,000. If 10% exceeds $75,000, ESC 16 receives the full percentage amount.
Scope
Texas revenue through ESC 16 contracting services only. Revenue from clients not using ESC 16 for contracting is excluded.
Out-of-State
Not included. Separate conversation after Texas terms are finalized.
Effective Date
January 1, 2026 (retroactive)
Term
Annual, January 1 – December 31. Renewal by mutual agreement.

Revenue Share Adjustments at Scale

When ESC 16's Annual Share Reaches Rate on Additional Revenue
$150,000 7.5%
$225,000 5.0%

Rates reset to 10% at the start of each contract year.

Contracting Services — ESC 16 Responsibilities

  • Quote development and proposal preparation
  • Purchase order processing
  • Contract execution with districts
  • Invoicing, billing, and payment collection
  • Payment remittance to Bookmarked
  • Renewal processing
  • 2 business day turnaround on quotes and invoicing

Marketing and Training Collaboration — Trial Year

  • Bookmarked provides monthly marketing content
  • ESC 16 distributes through agreed channels to member districts
  • Training collaboration to be explored as part of the partnership value
  • No financial penalties for Year 1
  • Quarterly review of activities and effectiveness

Year 1 establishes what marketing and training collaboration looks like. Performance expectations formalized in Year 2 renewal based on actual data.

Partner ESCs

  • ESC 16 manages all relationships with partner ESCs
  • Revenue splits with partner ESCs come from ESC 16's share
  • Bookmarked requests visibility into partner ESC arrangements to ensure relevant questions can be answered appropriately

Operational

  • Monthly check-in between Bookmarked and ESC 16 leadership
  • Bookmarked provides partnership dashboard covering revenue, pipeline, and activity
