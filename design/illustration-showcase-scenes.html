1. Compliance Shields

Six shield variations for SB 13 compliance messaging — pick your favorite

A: Classic Shield + Check

Heraldic shield with checkmark and book icon — strong compliance signal

B: Shield + Lock

Modern rounded shield with lock — data security and compliance

C: Compliance Badge

Circular badge with inner shield — "certified compliant" feel

D: Shield Over Open Book

Compliance shield protecting the book — "your collection is protected"

E: Minimal Outline Shield

Light, clean — works well at small sizes and on cards

F: Shield + Star Rating

Achievement/certification feel with pumpkin star — "gold standard" compliance
