# E1: Partnership Announcement — "We've Partnered with Bookmarked"

## How to Customize
- Replace **[ESC Name]** with your ESC (e.g., "Region 10 Education Service Center")
- Replace **[Region N]** with your region number
- Replace **[City]** with your ESC city
- Replace **[Recipient Name]** and **[District Name]** with the specific superintendent/district
- Replace **[Your Name]** and **[Your Title]** with the sender's info

---

**From:** [Your Name], [Your Title], [ESC Name]
**To:** Dr. [Recipient Name], Superintendent, [District Name] ISD

---

### Subject Line Options
1. A new resource for your library team
2. Partnership update from [Region N]
3. Something your librarians should know about

---

Dear Dr. [Recipient Name],

I wanted to share a partnership we've entered that I think will matter to your district.

We've partnered with Bookmarked — a Texas-based company that built a Book Intelligence platform specifically for the challenges our districts are facing. Over 150 Texas districts are already using their platform, OnShelf, to handle book review workflows, maintain defensible compliance records, and give their library teams better tools.

What stood out to us is that Bookmarked wasn't built as a generic library tool with compliance features added later. They built it from the ground up for what SB 13 and SB 412 actually require. Their Book Intelligence engine pulls together what public sources say about every title — challenge data, content context, peer district decisions — so your librarians aren't starting every review from scratch.

We've vetted them and we're recommending them to our member districts. We're not asking you to commit to anything — just wanted you to know this exists.

If you'd like to learn more, I'm happy to connect you with their team or walk you through what we've seen.

Warm regards,

[Your Name]
[Your Title]
