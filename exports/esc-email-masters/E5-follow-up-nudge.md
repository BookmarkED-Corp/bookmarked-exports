# E5: Follow-Up Nudge — "Checking In — Your Library Team Deserves This"

## How to Customize
- Replace **[ESC Name]** with your ESC (e.g., "Region 10 Education Service Center")
- Replace **[Region N]** with your region number
- Replace **[City]** with your ESC city
- Replace **[Recipient Name]** and **[District Name]** with the specific superintendent/district
- Replace **[Your Name]** and **[Your Title]** with the sender's info
- Personalize the opening — reference a previous conversation, email, or event if you can

---

**From:** [Your Name], [Your Title], [ESC Name]
