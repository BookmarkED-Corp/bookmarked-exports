Dear Dr. [Recipient Name],

I want to take a moment to explain not just what Bookmarked does, but why we partnered with them. The why matters.

**The real problem.** Yes, SB 13 and SB 412 created urgent challenges. But the deeper issue is that compliance is consuming what it was meant to protect. Librarians are managing spreadsheets instead of connecting students with books. Districts are hesitating on book orders because the regulatory risk feels too high. Libraries are getting quieter, more cautious, and less vital. That's backwards.

Libraries should be the most vibrant rooms in a school. That belief is what drew us to Bookmarked.

**Who they are.** Bookmarked is the Book Intelligence Company — a Texas-founded company that built its platform specifically for the challenges our districts face. OnShelf is used by over 150 Texas districts, and ESCs across the state — [LIST PARTNER REGIONS HERE — remove your own ESC from this list] — have partnered with them.

What makes them different is the approach. Bookmarked provides intelligence, not instructions. Data, not directives. They don't tell districts which books are appropriate — that judgment belongs to librarians, communities, and boards. What they do is ensure those decisions are informed by everything publicly known about a book, documented in a defensible system of record, and strengthened by the collective intelligence of Bookmarked's growing school library network in Texas.

**Why we chose them.** We were looking for a partner that understood the regulatory burden, respected librarians' professional judgment, provided real intelligence rather than just data management, had proven traction with Texas districts, and took a neutral approach that could serve every community in our region. Bookmarked checked every box.

**What this means for you.** We're not asking for a decision. We're asking you to take a look. If your library team is carrying a burden that feels unsustainable, if your board is asking questions you don't have great answers to, or if your librarians deserve better tools — Bookmarked is worth a conversation.

I'm here whenever you're ready.
