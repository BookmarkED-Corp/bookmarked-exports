Sample Instagram/LinkedIn Caption

Meet Paige Turner, Campus Librarian at Demo ISD.

With 12 years in school libraries, Paige has seen what happens when kids find the right book. "The best part of my job is watching a reluctant reader find the book that changes everything for them."

We asked Paige what excites her about the future of school libraries: "There's so much potential with the tools we have now. I can spend less time on paperwork and more time doing what I got into this profession for — connecting kids with stories that matter."

Thank you, Paige, for being a Library Voice.
